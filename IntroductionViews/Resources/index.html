SmartTime uses logic to look at your appointments, then find time for your tasks. It then combines both dynamically into a single view. SmartNotes
An intuitive way to organize your thoughts
 

SmartNotes allows you to quickly group and organize ideas, notes, to-do’s, lists, photos and even voice notes all from an intuitive desktop. Just drag an item with your fingertip onto another one, to group them together. The app also integrates with the iPhone’s Contact list, Google Maps, and Phone.

SmartPlans
Manage Anything, or Anyone
 

SmartPlans is four apps combined into one. It is a Project Planner, a Contact Manager, a Task Organizer and a Time Logger. Tasks are organized into structured Plans, and multiple Plans can be managed from an interactive Gantt-style view that allows you not only to keep up to date on your Plans, but also to maintain an effective Work Balance. The included Timer can be used to log anything from project tasks to phone calls to client meetings to workout schedules.
